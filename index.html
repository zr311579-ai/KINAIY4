About Us

KINAIYA is not just a name whispered in the halls of AMA Computer College–Makati. It is a pulse, a rhythm, a quiet fire that lives in the hearts of its Journalism Club members.

It is curiosity—the kind that leans in to listen when the world speaks softly. It is bravery—the courage to ask questions that matter, even when the answers are heavy. It is thoughtfulness—the care to notice the unseen, to honor the overlooked, to give voice to moments that might otherwise fade into silence.

More than rules or skills, KINAIYA is a way of being. It is care stitched into sentences, integrity carried in questions, honesty woven through every story. Each article is not just ink on paper, but a reflection of empathy, a bridge between truth and feeling.

KINAIYA reminds us that journalism is not only about reporting—it is about understanding. It is about standing in another's shoes, hearing the tremble in a voice, and translating that into words that connect. It is about weaving facts with compassion, so that stories do not just inform, but also move hearts.

In every whisper heard, in every truth told, KINAIYA is there—gentle yet unyielding, reminding us that storytelling is both a responsibility and a gift.

At AMA Makati, KINAIYA is more than the spirit of a club. It is the soul of a community, a promise that every story matters, and every voice deserves to be heard.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

We strive to be the beacon of student expression where every story is heard, every truth valued, and every voice sparks awareness, action, and change. We envision a community where no voice is lost, and student journalists grow both in craft and character, shaping narratives that enlighten minds, move hearts, and nurture positive transformation.

Mission

Kinaiya champions fearless journalism that speaks truth, sparks thought, and connects hearts. We exist to amplify student voices and transform words into impact. Through creative and honest storytelling, we aim to inform, inspire, and awaken understanding within our community.
